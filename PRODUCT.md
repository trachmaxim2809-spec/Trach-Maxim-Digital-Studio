# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Businesses and entrepreneurs looking for custom mobile apps, web development, and AI-integrated automation solutions.

## Product Purpose
A high-fidelity landing page and portfolio showcasing services, case studies, and capturing leads for Trach Maxim Digital Studio.

## Brand Personality
Premium, cutting-edge, professional.

## Anti-references
Generic templates, excessive emoji use, gray-on-gray low contrast text, cluttered grids.

## Design Principles
- **Premium simplicity**: Pure white canvas with precise royal blue highlights and clean typography.
- **Flawless layout consistency**: Standardized padding, alignment, and robust responsiveness from mobile to desktop.
- **Value-oriented showcase**: Clear feature grids, structured service lists, and detailed interactive cases.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA contrast ratio.
- Keyboard navigable controls and descriptive aria-labels.
- Large touch targets (minimum 44x44px).
